Netflix acquired the global streaming rights in late 2017. It re-cut the series into 22 shorter episodes and released them worldwide, beginning with the first part on December 20, 2017, followed by the second part on April 6, 2018. In April 2018, Netflix renewed the series with a significantly increased budget for 16 new episodes total. Warning: Contains graphic violence involving children.
